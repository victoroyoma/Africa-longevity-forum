About the Forum

Why Africa Longevity Forum Matters

Africa Longevity Forum 2026 is a premier two-day gathering focused on the future of health resilience, longevity science, AI-driven healthcare systems, indigenous biodiversity, biotechnology, workforce wellness, and sustainable economic transformation across Africa.

The forum creates a platform for scientists, investors, governments, universities, health institutions, young innovators, biotech startups, environmental researchers, and development organizations to collaborate on scalable solutions that improve lifespan, productivity, and economic resilience across the continent.

Organised by AFROLONGEVITY, the International Longevity Summit Africa (ILSA), and TAFFD's — Transdisciplinary Agora for Future Discussions — this is Africa's most important convergence of longevity science and economic thinking.

🧬
World-Class Science
Cutting-edge research in genomics, AI biomarkers, precision medicine, and longevity therapeutics tailored for African populations.
💡
Policy & Investment
Direct dialogue between policymakers, investors, and innovators to create actionable roadmaps for national health and economic resilience.
🌍
Pan-African Collaboration
Connecting delegates from 15+ countries to build the cross-border partnerships that will define Africa's wellness economy.
🚀
Youth & Future Leadership
Dedicated platforms for young African scientists and futurists to present innovations and enter the global longevity ecosystem.
